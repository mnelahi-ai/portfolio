Continuity & Insulation Resistance Test Sheet

Common practice: 500 V DC test for 415 V systems, min 1 MΩ field acceptance (higher for new cables — many specs require ≥100 MΩ at FAT); 1 kV/2.5 kV/5 kV for HV per spec. Disconnect electronics (VSDs, AMS I/O, nav equipment) before testing. Record ambient temperature; IR halves roughly every 10 °C rise.

Attach raw test records (IR sheets, injection results, load test logs) to this certificate. One certificate per system/test scope.
