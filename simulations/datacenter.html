Design Logic Demonstrated

This model applies the same principles I commission in the field: 2N path separation (no shared single point of failure between A and B), UPS ride-through sized for generator start and transfer (10-minute battery at full load), ATS transfer of the MV boards to standby generation on dual utility loss, and dual-corded load behaviour — the IT load stays online while at least one cord is healthy. Availability is logged against the 99.999% (five-nines) target: 5.26 minutes of allowable downtime per year.
