Dispatch Rules

1 · Renewables serve the load first.
2 · Surplus charges the BESS (≤ 60 kW, ≤ 95% SOC).
3 · Deficit discharges the BESS (≤ 60 kW, ≥ 15% SOC).
4 · Genset auto-starts on SOC ≤ 20% or deficit > BESS rating; runs ≥ 30% load, recharges BESS with spare capacity.
5 · Turbine cuts out above 25 m/s; load shed only if all sources exhausted.

Engineering Basis

Mirrors the autonomous solar-wind remote power plant I engineered at Phoenix Mecano — hybrid generation with UPS/BESS backbone and SCADA supervision under IECEx/ATEX discipline. Wind output follows a cubic power curve between cut-in and rated speed; PV output scales with irradiance at 20% system efficiency; genset fuel burn is modelled at 0.27 L/kWh with a 30% minimum-load rule to avoid wet-stacking. Frequency deviation indicates instantaneous supply-demand imbalance — sustained imbalance triggers load shed, the microgrid equivalent of losing the critical bus.
