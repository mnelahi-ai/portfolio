METHOD & BASIS
Analysis basis: single-failure concept of the 2000 HSC Code, Annex 3 / Annex 4 (failure-mode worksheet with sea-trial test references). Severity classes — Minor, Major, Hazardous, Catastrophic; probability classes — Frequent to Extremely Improbable, per the approved document. The vessel's redundancy philosophy (twin independent trains, quad-redundant 24 VDC control power, back-up & indication lanes, hydraulic emergency operation) means no single failure analysed leaves the vessel without propulsion and steering.
