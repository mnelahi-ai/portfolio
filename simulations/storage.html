Thesis Takeaway

Short-duration systems (Li-ion, ≤4 h) excel at daily solar shifting and EV-charging peaks; long-duration technologies (pumped hydro, flow, hydrogen, CAES) are what decarbonise multi-day wind lulls — but at lower round-trip efficiency and higher energy-capacity cost. No single technology wins: portfolios matched to the renewable profile of each country performed best in the reviewed literature.
